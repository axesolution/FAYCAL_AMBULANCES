# **App Name**: Faycal Ambulance Services

## Core Features:

- Services Overview: A comprehensive section detailing ambulance services, emergency transport, and funeral transport options available in Algiers.
- About Us: Dedicated page outlining the company's mission, values, and commitment to professional and compassionate service.
- Contact Information & Inquiry Form: Clear and easily accessible contact details including phone numbers, email, and a simple inquiry form for non-urgent communication.
- Emergency Call-to-Action: A prominently displayed, sticky call-to-action button for immediate emergency contact, ensuring rapid response.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary (elements/buttons): Deep, professional dark blue (#0F36AA), representing trust and reliability.
- Background: Very dark desaturated blue (#1C1E23), providing a solemn and reassuring base.
- Text/Accent: Crisp white (#FFFFFF) and light gray 'silver' (#BFBFBF) for readability and contrast against dark backgrounds.
- Accent (Subtle Red): A muted, subtle red (#B23E3E) used sparingly for critical call-to-action buttons or alerts to denote urgency without being overwhelming.
- Headlines and body text: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, neutral, and highly readable quality, ensuring clear communication of important information.
- Clean, minimalist line icons used for services and features, conveying professionalism and clarity related to medical and transport themes.
- Structured and spacious layout with ample negative space to emphasize key information and maintain a calming, professional atmosphere.
- Subtle and smooth transitions on hover states for buttons and navigation, enhancing user experience without distraction.
